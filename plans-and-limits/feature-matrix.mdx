## Understanding scan presets

Most scanning tools offer two intensity levels:

- **Light** – Faster scans using passive detection techniques on common ports
- **Deep** – Comprehensive scans with active detection on extended port ranges

<Info>
Light scans are available on all plans including the free tier. Deep scans unlock more thorough vulnerability detection and are available on paid plans based on tool category.
</Info>
