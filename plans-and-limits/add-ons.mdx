## Overview

Add-ons are premium features that extend your Pentest-Tools.com subscription beyond the standard plan capabilities. They're available for purchase alongside any paid plan.

## Available add-ons

### VPN add-on

The VPN add-on enables scanning of internal networks and targets that aren't accessible from the public internet. This includes:

- Scanning private IP ranges (10.x.x.x, 172.16.x.x, 192.168.x.x)
- Accessing targets behind corporate firewalls
- Reaching staging, development, or isolated network environments

<Info>
For complete VPN setup instructions, deployment options, and troubleshooting, see the [VPN profiles documentation](/using/vpn/overview).
</Info>

### White Label add-on

The White Label add-on lets you customize the appearance of reports and emails with your own branding. This includes:

- Custom logos on PDF and Word reports
- Branded email notifications
- Professional presentation for client deliverables

<Info>
For detailed configuration steps and branding options, see the [Branded reports documentation](/using/reporting/branded-reports).
</Info>

## Purchasing add-ons

Add-ons are priced as a percentage of your base subscription cost: VPN adds 20% and White Label adds 25%. When both are combined, the total increase is 45%. They can be added at any time to an active subscription.

### Adding to a new subscription

When subscribing to a paid plan:

1. Select your plan (NetSec, WebNetSec, or Pentest Suite)
2. Choose your asset tier
3. Select desired add-ons (VPN, White Label, or both)
4. Complete checkout with the combined total

### Adding to an existing subscription

To add an add-on to your current subscription:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your active plan">
    Navigate to **My Account > Active plan**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Change add-ons">
    Click **Change add-ons** and select the add-on you want to add.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review pricing">
    The add-on cost is a percentage of your current plan price (20% for VPN, 25% for White Label). Charges are prorated for the remainder of your billing period.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm purchase">
    Complete the checkout to activate your add-on immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
VPN adds 20% and White Label adds 25% to your base plan price. When combined, the total increase is 45%.
</Note>

## Managing add-ons

### Viewing active add-ons

Check your current add-ons in **My Account > Active plan**. You'll see:

- Which add-ons are active
- When each add-on renews
- Add-on costs on your subscription

### Removing an add-on

To remove an add-on from your subscription:

1. Go to **My Account > Active plan**
2. Click **Change add-ons**
3. Deselect the add-on you want to remove
4. Confirm the change

<Info>
**Upgrades are instant and prorated**: When you add an add-on, it activates immediately and you're charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing period.

**Removals are scheduled**: When you remove an add-on, it remains active until the end of your current billing period. The add-on will not renew on your next billing date.
</Info>

<Warning>
When the VPN add-on is removed at the end of your billing period, all VPN profiles are disabled and workspaces are unlinked from VPN profiles. Download any configurations you need before the removal takes effect.
</Warning>

<Warning>
When the White Label add-on is removed, all reports and emails revert to default Pentest-Tools.com branding.
</Warning>
