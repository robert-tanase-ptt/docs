## Adding a Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to Settings → Billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add Payment">
    Select "Add Payment Method".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Card Details">
    Provide card number, expiration, and CVV.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify">
    Complete any verification required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Payment Methods

### Default Payment Method

Your default payment method is used for:
- Subscription renewals
- Add-on purchases
- Overage charges

### Updating Card Details

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Find your payment method
3. Click Edit
4. Update details
5. Save changes

<Warning>
Update your payment method before your card expires to avoid service interruption.
</Warning>

### Removing Payment Methods

1. Ensure another payment method is default
2. Click Remove on the card to delete
3. Confirm removal

## Billing Information

### Invoice Details

Provide billing information for invoices:
- Company name
- Billing address
- Tax ID (if applicable)

### Updating Billing Info

1. Go to Settings → Billing
2. Click "Billing Information"
3. Update fields
4. Save

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payment declined">
    - Verify card details are correct
    - Check available credit/funds
    - Contact your bank if persists
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Card expired">
    - Add a new payment method
    - Update card expiration date
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
