## Viewing Invoices

Access your invoices:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to Settings → Billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invoices">
    Select the "Invoices" or "Billing History" tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Invoice">
    Click any invoice to view details.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invoice Information

Each invoice includes:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Invoice Number | Unique identifier |
| Date | Invoice date |
| Period | Billing period covered |
| Items | Services and quantities |
| Amount | Total charged |
| Status | Paid, pending, failed |

## Downloading Invoices

### Single Invoice

1. Find the invoice in your history
2. Click Download or PDF icon
3. Save the PDF file

### Bulk Download

Some plans allow bulk invoice download:
1. Select date range
2. Click "Download All"
3. Receive ZIP file with PDFs

## Invoice Status

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Paid | Successfully processed |
| Pending | Awaiting payment |
| Failed | Payment unsuccessful |
| Refunded | Amount returned |

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:

<Warning>
Failed payments may result in service interruption. Update your payment method promptly.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    We send notification of failed payment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Payment">
    Add or update your payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Retry Payment">
    System will retry or you can pay manually.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Custom Invoice Requirements

### Adding Tax ID

1. Go to Settings → Billing → Billing Information
2. Add your VAT/Tax ID
3. Save changes
4. Future invoices will include it

### Company Details

Update billing information to appear on invoices:
- Company name
- Billing address
- Tax identification

## Enterprise Invoicing

Enterprise customers may have:
- NET 30 payment terms
- Purchase order references
- Custom invoice formatting
- Consolidated billing

Contact your account manager for enterprise billing needs.

## Dispute or Questions

For invoice questions:
1. Contact billing support
2. Include invoice number
3. Describe the issue
4. Provide any documentation

<Note>
Disputes should be raised within 30 days of the invoice date.
</Note>
