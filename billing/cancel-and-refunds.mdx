## Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to Settings â†’ Billing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    Select "Cancel Subscription".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Feedback">
    Optionally tell us why you're leaving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm Cancellation">
    Confirm to complete the cancellation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Happens After Cancellation

| Aspect | What Happens |
|--------|--------------|
| Access | Continues until period ends |
| Data | Retained for 30 days |
| Renewal | No future charges |
| Reactivation | Can resubscribe anytime |

<Note>
Your subscription remains active until the end of your current billing period.
</Note>

## Data After Cancellation

### Retention Period

- Account data retained for 30 days
- Can export data before deletion
- Resubscribing restores access

### Permanent Deletion

After 30 days:
- Scan results deleted
- Findings removed
- Assets cleared
- Account anonymized

<Warning>
Export any data you need before your retention period expires.
</Warning>

## Refund Policy

### Eligible for Refund

- Within 14 days of purchase
- No significant usage
- First-time subscriptions

### Not Eligible

- After 14-day period
- Significant platform usage
- Renewal charges (can cancel instead)
- Add-on purchases

## Requesting a Refund

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact Support">
    Email support with your refund request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Details">
    Include account email and reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review">
    We'll review your request.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Processing">
    Refunds processed within 5-10 business days.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Pausing Instead of Canceling

If you need a temporary break:

<Tip>
Consider downgrading to the Free Edition instead of canceling to maintain your account and data.
</Tip>

## Reactivating

After cancellation, you can resubscribe:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Billing
3. Choose a plan
4. Complete payment

If within 30 days, your previous data may be restored.
