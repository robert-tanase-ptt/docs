## Changing Your Plan

You can change your subscription plan at any time.

## Upgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to Settings → Billing → Plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose the plan you want to upgrade to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Changes">
    See what features you'll gain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Complete the upgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Upgrade Billing

- **Immediate access**: New features available right away
- **Prorated charges**: Pay the difference for the current period
- **Next renewal**: Full new plan price

## Downgrading

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to Settings → Billing → Plans.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose the lower tier plan.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Impact">
    Understand what you'll lose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Schedule the downgrade.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Downgrade Considerations

<Warning>
Downgrading may affect your access to features, assets, and data.
</Warning>

| Impact | What Happens |
|--------|--------------|
| Extra assets | May need to remove some |
| Scheduled scans | May be disabled |
| Team members | May lose access |
| History | Some data may be limited |

### When Downgrade Takes Effect

- Takes effect at next billing cycle
- Current features remain until then
- Plan your transition

## Plan Change Timeline

| Action | When It Happens |
|--------|-----------------|
| Upgrade | Immediate |
| Downgrade | Next billing cycle |
| Cancel | End of current period |

## Billing Adjustments

### Upgrades

You're charged the prorated difference immediately, then the full new price at next renewal.

### Downgrades

Current period continues at current price. New lower price starts at next renewal.

## Before Changing Plans

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Review current usage">
    Check that the new plan meets your needs.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Export data if downgrading">
    Export any data that might be affected.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Notify team members">
    If reducing seats, coordinate with your team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
