## Security certification

Pentest-Tools.com is ISO 27001 certified. The certification covers the information security management system for the platform and its underlying infrastructure.

## Data processing

### Data retention

We retain personal data for up to 24 months. Financial records are kept for 10 years as required by law. For full details, see our [privacy policy](https://pentest-tools.com/legal/privacy-policy).

### Data deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, contact [data.privacy@pentest-tools.com](mailto:data.privacy@pentest-tools.com). You can also export your scan data for up to 30 days after account termination.

## AI data policy

Our platform includes AI-powered features: the [Soft 404 detector](/ai/soft404detector), [endpoint discovery](/ai/endpoint-discovery), and [AI-enhanced authentication](/ai/authentication).

### Our AI infrastructure

Our AI features use different models depending on the task:

- Proprietary models for classification tasks like soft 404 detection, hosted on our own infrastructure (Linode for servers, Vast.ai for model hosting)
- Azure-hosted models for browser-based AI features like endpoint discovery and authentication, running within our controlled Azure infrastructure

All infrastructure partners are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your data for any purpose beyond providing the service.

### Data handling

Your scan data is not used to train any AI models, whether proprietary or third-party. We do not log or retain your data for AI-related debugging, and there is no retention window for AI processing. Your data is not shared with any party outside the infrastructure needed to run the feature.

### Model training

Our foundational models were trained on public data and de-identified, aggregated historical user data. We are not currently training new models. If we develop future AI features that could use your data, we will say so clearly and give you a way to opt out.

### About AI accuracy

Our AI features do not hallucinate. Our models classify data rather than create it, and each runs inside a rule-based pipeline. If something goes wrong, the scan continues without it. A failure means a missed classification. Not an invention.

## Responsible disclosure

If you find a security vulnerability in our platform, email [security@pentest-tools.com](mailto:security@pentest-tools.com). Include a description of the issue and steps to reproduce it. We follow responsible disclosure practices and will keep you updated on the fix.
