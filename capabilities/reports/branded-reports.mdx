Add your company logo to reports and configure how they are delivered by email. These settings apply to all reports you generate.

<Info>
This feature requires the **White Label add-on**. See [Add-ons](/account-billing/plans-and-limits/add-ons) for pricing and how to enable it on your subscription.
</Info>

## Custom logo

Replace the default logo in your reports with your own company branding.

### Uploading a logo

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to logo settings">
    Go to **Reports > Settings > Logo Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your logo">
    Click to upload your company logo image.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Adjust positioning">
    Configure margins to position the logo correctly on the report cover page:
    - **Margin Top**
    - **Margin Bottom**
    - **Margin Left**
    - **Margin Right**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save settings">
    Save your logo settings. The logo will appear on all future reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Your logo appears on the cover page of PDF and DOCX reports, replacing the default Pentest-Tools.com logo.
</Note>

### Removing a custom logo

To revert to the default logo, delete your custom logo from the Logo Settings page.

## Email settings

Customize how report delivery emails appear to recipients.

### Available settings

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Subject** | Email subject line (supports dynamic tags) |
| **From (name)** | Sender name shown to recipients |
| **Reply-to Email** | Address recipients reply to |
| **Reply-to Name** | Name shown for reply-to |
| **Introduction** | Opening text in the email body |
| **Signature** | Closing signature in the email body |

### Dynamic tags

Use these tags in the email subject to insert dynamic values:

| Tag | Inserts |
|-----|---------|
| `{{ TOOL }}` | The tool name that generated the scan |
| `{{ TARGET }}` | The scanned target |
| `{{ DATE }}` | The report generation date |

### Configuring email settings

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to email settings">
    Go to **Reports > Settings > Email Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize fields">
    Fill in your preferred subject line, sender name, reply-to details, introduction, and signature.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save settings">
    Save your email settings. They'll be used for all report delivery emails.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Include your company name in the "From (name)" field so recipients immediately recognize the email.
</Tip>

<Info>
Want to send emails from your own domain instead of the default? Contact support to set up custom email domains.
</Info>

<Tip>
The White Label add-on also sends branded email notifications for scan completions and scheduled reports. It can be added to any paid plan (NetSec, WebNetSec, or Pentest Suite).
</Tip>
