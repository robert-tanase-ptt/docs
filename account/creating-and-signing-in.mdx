## Creating an Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Registration">
    Go to app.pentest-tools.com and click "Sign Up".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide your email address and create a password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Click the verification link sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Add any additional required information.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing In

### Standard Login

1. Go to app.pentest-tools.com
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

### Single Sign-On (SSO)

Enterprise accounts may have SSO configured:
1. Click "Sign in with SSO"
2. Enter your organization domain
3. Authenticate with your identity provider

## Password Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Minimum length | 8 characters |
| Complexity | Mix of letters, numbers, symbols |
| History | Cannot reuse recent passwords |

## Forgot Password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Forgot Password">
    On the login page, click "Forgot password?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email">
    Provide your account email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email">
    Click the reset link in the email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set New Password">
    Create a new secure password.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Password reset links expire after 24 hours.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Not receiving verification email">
    - Check spam/junk folder
    - Verify email address spelling
    - Request new verification email
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Account locked">
    - Wait for lockout period
    - Use password reset
    - Contact support if persistent
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
